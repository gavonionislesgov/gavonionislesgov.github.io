Government

The Gavonion Isles are a Constitutional Republic, led by the Grand Chancellor and the People's Council.

Excerpt from our Constitution:

"All citizens of the Gavonion Isles are equal under the sun and stars. Creativity shall be our compass; freedom, our destination."

Citizenship

Citizenship is open to all who share our values of imagination, freedom, and community.

Types of Citizenship:

  • Explorer: New citizen, learning our ways.
  • Navigator: Active citizen contributing to projects.
  • Guardian: Citizen with leadership and community service roles.
