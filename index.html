About

Founded in 2025, the Gavonion Isles were created to offer freedom, art, and wisdom. Our founders dreamed of a place without borders, full of possibilities.

Geography

Seven lush islands with jungles, beaches, and hidden coves await your discovery.

Economy

Our economy thrives on creativity, arts, and tourism. We trade crafts and ideas, inspiring all who visit.

Military

While we cherish peace, our Royal Naval Guard ensures our safety with maritime defense.

Culture

Music and art are central to our culture. The Festival of Stars lights up the sky each year for three magical nights.
